Privacy Policy

Crane stores account, application, chat, and audit information in persistent server-side storage so borrower and admin activity remains available across restarts and future updates.

What We Store

  • Registration details such as phone number, email, and profile information.
  • Loan applications, approval decisions, repayment records, and notifications.
  • Support chat messages, audit logs, and risk-management events for operational review.

How We Use Data

  • To assess eligibility, review documents, and service approved facilities.
  • To coordinate borrower, admin, and master-admin actions within the platform.
  • To investigate risk alerts, maintain compliance records, and recover account access when needed.

Security

Crane uses authenticated access controls, persistent server-side storage, and audit logging to reduce accidental data loss and support safe operational review.
